How It Started

A love for dressing
well — made into a brand.

Larry B began with a young woman who had always loved to look good. Long before she called herself a fashion designer, she was already living it — dressing with care, noticing how the right outfit could shift the way a woman carries herself, and dreaming of building something that gave other women that same feeling.

That dream became LARRY B — a brand born not from a business plan, but from a genuine, deep-rooted passion for style, elegance, and the quiet confidence that comes with dressing well.

Today, Larry B is driven by that same energy: exquisite pieces, a love for nature and beauty, and the belief that every woman deserves to feel remarkable in what she wears.

Frequently Asked Questions

Larry B carries sizes S through XXL across most styles. Some custom and limited pieces may vary. If you're unsure about sizing, feel free to reach out via Instagram or email and we'll help you find your perfect fit.
Yes — custom orders are available for select styles. Send us a message with your measurements and what you have in mind and we'll let you know what's possible. Custom orders typically take 7–14 business days.
Delivery within Lagos typically takes 1–3 business days. Orders outside Lagos are delivered within 3–7 business days depending on your location. You'll receive a confirmation once your order has been dispatched.
We accept exchanges within 7 days of delivery for items that are unworn, unwashed, and in original condition. Please contact us before sending anything back so we can guide you through the process.
Most pieces are best hand-washed or put on a gentle machine cycle in cold water. Avoid bleach and tumble drying to preserve the fabric and fit. Care instructions are included with every order.
